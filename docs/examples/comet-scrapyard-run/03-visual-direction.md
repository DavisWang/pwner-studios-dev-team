# Visual Direction Brief

## Style Pillars

- industrial salvage, not clean sci-fi
- readable silhouettes over dense detail
- high contrast between collectible scrap, hazards, and safe routes

## Interface Priorities

- cargo load and hull must read at a glance
- refinery cash-in state needs a strong audiovisual payoff
- hazard warnings should flare before impact

## Sensory Direction

- crunchy magnet pull audio
- bright refinery pulse when cashing in
- short screen shake on heavy collisions

## Animation Direction

- Keep motion in the same industrial salvage style as the rest of the brief.
- If the tug, scrap, or refinery payoff needs extra frames or sprites to sell the motion, generate them in-style rather than switching to line art or placeholder motion language.

## Risks

- too much particle noise will hurt readability
- similar colors for scrap and hazards will weaken decision speed
